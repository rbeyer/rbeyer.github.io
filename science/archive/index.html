Ross A. Beyer's Publication Archive

To get a PDF or HTML version of the published papers, follow the Self-Archived Full Text links. Each paper has a section noting how it is different from the published version.

I recommend publishing your papers in an open access journal, but if you, like me, published some papers in journals without that feature, you may be able to self-archive your papers even if you have signed over the copyrights by carefully looking at the rights that the journal provides back to you as an author.
